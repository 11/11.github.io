Introduction

Okay so here's the beat. When I first tried to seriously learn VIm, it was difficult to find just one good source that listed out the essentials tools to navigate VIm; and what I mean by essentials does not involve giving a tutorial explaining how to open VIm and go into insert mode, but rather how to navigate effectively and edit specific components to a line of code. So without a further ado, I present: 15 VIm Commands You Should Know

Disclaimer

Just a quick heads up, by know means am I writing this article with a self-righteous mindset - rather I'm trying state my own thoughts on what is a subjective topic. Also, as for the logistics of this article, I'm assuming you know the basics to VIm - as in how to open a file, and how to navigate within Normal and Insert mode.

Movement

The first thing to learn about in VIm is simply how to navigate. Truly, the fluidity that you feel when you move the cursor in VIm is really something else. Never have I had this much control in any other editor, hence why I preach using VIm to everyone. So lets start with the basics shall we!
