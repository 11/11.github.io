About

Hello, my name is Doug. I'm a former student from Rutgers University - New Brunswick with a degree in computer science.

I'm currently living in the greater New York City Area working as an Infrastructure Engineer at BuzzFeed as well as an open source contributor for Epic Games' Unreal Game Engine.

I enjoy doing lots of things like photography, giving talks, making video games, and beer tasting.

In my spare time, I also like mentoring students and teaching computer science. Feel free to email me, I would love to get to know you more.
