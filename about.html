About

Hello, my name is Doug. I'm a recent graduate from Rutgers University - New Brunswick with a degree in computer science.

I'm currently living in the greater New York City Area working as an Infrastructure Engineer at BuzzFeed as well as an open source contributor for Epic Games' Unreal Game Engine.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy doing lots of things like photography, making video games, playing sports, and trying new food.

In my spare time, I also like mentoring students and teaching potential software developers computer science. Feel free to email me, I would love to get to know you more.
