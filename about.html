About

Hello, my name is Doug.

I code at BuzzFeed as a backend engineer in the greater NYC area. I'm currently working towards a new education initiative that is to create an open VR platform. The goal is to allow educators to develop interactive demos that will enable students to experience their education.

In my down time, I like to mentor students in the NYC tech scene, write blogs, and create animation demos. In the past, I worked on Unreal Game Engine's open websocket library and also interned at BuzzFeed.

I was also a hackNY fellow in 2017 - the summer I interened at BuzzFeed.
