About

I'm Doug, I'm a recent graduate from Rutgers University - New Brunswick with a degree in computer science.

I'm currently living in the greater New York City Area working at BuzzFeed - some of you might have heard of it. Right now, I'm working as an Infrastructure Engineer with intentions to move towards graphics related engineering in the near future.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy doing lots of things like learning, making video games, and playing sports ... literally any sport.

Lastly, I enjoy mentoring students and teaching potential software developers in computer science, so please feel free to email me.

Ultimately, I aspire to build experiences for myself and others that are humorous yet (hopefully) useful to people like you.

Look forward to hearing from you.
